COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.

The disease causes respirtory illness (like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough, fever, and in more severe cases , difficulty breathing. You can protect yourself by washing your hands frequently, avoiding touching your face and avoiding close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell.

How it spreads?

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touchs a surface or object that has a virus on it, the touches their eyes, nose or mouth.
